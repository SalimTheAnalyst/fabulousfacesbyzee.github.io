About Zee

Empowering women through the art of beauty.

Based in Abuja, Nigeria, I started Fabulous Faces By Zee with one goal: to demystify makeup for the everyday woman. I believe makeup isn't about hiding who you are, but highlighting your best features and building the confidence to take on the world.

With years of experience in bridal, glam, and everyday natural looks, I’ve realized that many ladies find makeup intimidating. That’s why I focus on beginner-friendly techniques that deliver professional results.
